VitaSleeve is an integrated robotic life-support system delivering continuous care, mobility, and autonomy. Fully built units are for sale, and all designs, software, and electronics are open-source for DIY makers.

What is VitaSleeve?

VitaSleeve is a wearable, full-body exosuit with onboard medical infrastructure, prosthetic robotics, and autonomous assistance. It is built for people with chronic conditions, disabilities, or mobility challenges. The project is open-source: view schematics, code, and 3D-printable parts.

Core Systems

  • Robotic Exosuit: Full-body strength support and prosthetic motion. 3D-printable parts
  • Neural Interface: Non-invasive control via neural decoding. Control software
  • Medical Infrastructure: Vitals monitoring, micro dialysis, insulin delivery. Circuit schematics
  • Emergency Suite: Auto bleeding control, infection detection.
  • Surgical & Imaging: Micro-surgical arm, light-MRI, AI diagnostics.
  • Autonomous Humanoid Mode: Operates as a standalone assistant for lifting and home tasks.

The Mission

Healthcare should not depend on buildings or distance. VitaSleeve brings care directly to the person who needs it. Our mission: protect life, restore independence, and empower humans. Learn more about building your own: Open-source resources.
